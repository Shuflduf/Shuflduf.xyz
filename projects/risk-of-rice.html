Risk of Rice

My first linux rice, based around the second best video game to ever exist. (Risk of Rain 2 is the first)

So I was playing Risk of Rain Returns an unhealthy amount, as one does, and I thought of how good a rice based on it's ui would be. So I started reasearching (playing Risk of Rain Returns and sometimes taking screenshots) and made a Figma sheet

Development

At first, I wanted to use Waybar, but then I reliazed it would be a very boring project, and a friend of mine introduced me to Quickshell, as it allowed me to do a lot more things than Waybar. Although I'm not currently using this rice, I'm stilll using Quickshell.

Then like 20 hours or so later I had the final result! A complete rice that installed a bunch of programs and their configs, so it was basically a personalized version of Arch by the end.

Features

  • Full hyprland integration (workspaces and stuff)
  • Integration with music players
  • Managing power profiles
  • Wallpaper manager
  • Bluetooth and Wifi and sound settings
  • A bunch of other hypr ecosystem stuff (hypridle, hyprlock, hyprpaper, hyprpaper, hyprpicker)
  • A bunch of cli and tui stuff (pacsea, fish, lazygit, yazi, helix)

Stack

  • Quickshell
  • hyprland
  • QML
  • Javascript
  • Bash
