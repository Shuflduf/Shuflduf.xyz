Remixed

The website for Remixed, a Hack Club YSWS, where You Ship a music related project, and We Ship music related stuff!

This started off when my pookie got on the team, and they needed to find someone for the website, so I was referred into the team! (Thanks pookie!)

I knew I had to cook, so I didn't do anything but work on the site for weeks. I completely stopped paying any attention in classes, and there were days where I barely ate just to work on the site.

This was my first real project, and it felt like everything before this was just practice. Needless to say, I cooked.

Obviously I didn't make it all alone, we had an entire team working to make it perfect, but I don't think anyone put in more work into the website than I did.

Towards the end, a designer locked in and made the peakest designs anyone has ever seen. Like damn they were GOOD. VERY hard to implement but still very good.
