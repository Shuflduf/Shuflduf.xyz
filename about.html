About This Site

Why?

Making a personal site where I talk about myself feels stupid. However, I do need a place to get my thoughts out on the internet somehow, but social media feels too restricive, and making a site is healthier for my programming brain.

What got me started on this was getting a free .xyz domain, as well as Flavortown, but since I started I got a bunch of ideas and will be implementing them, probably even after Flavortown ends.

Inspiration

The Indie Web was a big inspiration, and I'm constantly referencing sites from Neocities for style ideas. I have an entire design document full of little details to add to make it look authentic.

I was also inspired by Deltarune to have the same content work perfectly fine in two completely different styles, where one is styled after the Indie Web, and one looks like a normal portfolio site where I glaze myself to potential employers.
